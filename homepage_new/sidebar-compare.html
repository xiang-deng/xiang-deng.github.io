Sidebar Refinement

Two ways to make the profile area blend better

The issue is not the content itself, but the amount of enclosure around it. Here the nav keeps the balanced frosted treatment, while the sidebar becomes quieter and more integrated.

A. Integrated rail

Most blended

Removes the card feel entirely. The profile becomes a structured left rail with just a divider, so it reads as part of the layout rather than a featured object.
