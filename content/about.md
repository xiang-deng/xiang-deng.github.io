*Hi*, I am the Co-Founder and CTO of NeoCognition, where I build AI systems and agents with large language models. My current work focuses on coding, knowledge work, and personal agents that can coordinate across tools and services, automate substantial parts of execution, and operate under human supervision.

Before NeoCognition, I was a Senior Research Scientist at Scale AI and a Research Engineer at Google Labs. I have also worked with [Google Research](https://research.google/), [Amazon](https://www.amazon.science/), [Microsoft Research](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/research/group/natural-language-processing/people/), and Microsoft Research Asia, building large language models, agents, and practical AI systems.

I received my Ph.D. from [the OSU NLP Group](https://x.com/osunlp?s=09), where I was advised by Dr. [Huan Sun](http://web.cse.ohio-state.edu/~sun.397/) and worked closely with Dr. [Yu Su](https://ysu1989.github.io/). Before that, I earned a B.Eng. in Computer Science from [USTC](https://www.ustc.edu).

## Research

My research interests lie in NLP and AI broadly, with an emphasis on integrating knowledge from heterogeneous sources and building practical AI systems for both regular users and domain experts. Specifically, my research focuses on:

- **Large-scale pretraining and post-training** on heterogeneous data, including plain text, structured data, code, and images, for both general and domain-specific applications, and for learning beyond next-token prediction.
- **Natural language agents** that operate across diverse data, services, and environments, with an emphasis on practical, collaborative, efficient, and robust systems.

My work was among the early efforts to extend language modeling beyond plain text and to study generalist LM agents in real-world web environments. More broadly, I am interested in a new mode of work in which agents handle large parts of execution while people remain in the loop. In the spirit of "read ten thousand books and travel ten thousand miles" (`读万卷书，行万里路`), I am interested in unlocking the next generation of agent intelligence by enabling systems to explore, act, and learn from experience.
